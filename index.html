Red Door Cafe Rules

This is not a restaurant, this is an experience. So please come with an open mind, a kind heart and a fun/playful personality and I promise you a trip on my flamboyant Love Boat to heaven.

XO XO

The Owner


Suggestions on how to catch the love boat:

No parties of threes and no more than 2 due to limited seating capacity

Please do not take yourself so seriously, life is too short. If you can't play then go somewhere else. America needs your business.

Please do not block the sidewalk (our neighbors, seniors, wheel chairs and dog owners come first) - so please stand in line and be patient.

No pushy, entitled and alpha spoilt "mama's boys" or angry barbie/cheerleader wannabe girls. They scare me and I shut down my gushing love fountain. Remember, if I can't love you - I can't feed you.

No "ice water with lemon" and no "just hot water" It is cheap, suburban and annoying.

No egg-white orders. If you alter our menu, you will be spanked and sent home to cook for yourself.

If you give off bad energy vibes while waiting because quote: "I am a paying customer" and you expect the world to bend over for you, I will ask you to leave.

You will only be seated if all your party is present.

Most Important Rule:

Please, Please, Please Pretty Please do not bring your own coffee or coffee like drinks and stand in line. We are a cafe. We will gladly sell you a sexy cup of coffee while you wait.
